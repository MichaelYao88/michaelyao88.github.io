What is the difference between margin, border, and padding? Margin is space between the object and other objects, border is between margin and padding and can be made dashed or solid or whatever. padding is between border and content

What are the best practices associated with using classes vs. ids? classes for multiple things you'll address, ids for very specific things you want to change What are the differences between relative, absolute, fixed, and static positioning? static is where the html says it will be, relative is a new position relative to its static version, absolute is a selected area on the page where it will always be and fixed is a selected page where it will always be even if you scroll

What does it mean to display inline vs using inline block? inline only takes up the space of its content and will always place horizontally regardless of objects to its top or bottom. Inline block takes up a block of space and will respect both horizontal and vertical objects
